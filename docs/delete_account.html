Overview

You can request deletion of your BADGR Bolt account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to request deletion and exactly what data is removed.

If you use BADGR Bolt as a free user without an account, no data is stored on our servers and no action is required.

Before You Delete

⚠ This action is permanent

Account deletion cannot be undone. Your cloud-synced library, reading progress, and account credentials will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

Local data stored on your device — imported books, covers, and reading position — is not affected by account deletion and remains on your device until you uninstall the app.

How to Delete Your Account

1
Open BADGR Bolt on your Android device.
2
Tap the Account tab in the bottom navigation bar.
3
Confirm you are signed in to the account you wish to delete.
4
If you cannot access the app, email us at privacy@badgrtechnologies.com with the subject line Account Deletion Request and the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.
