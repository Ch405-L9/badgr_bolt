Privacy Policy
BADGR Bolt

Effective date: 2026-03-14  ·  Last updated: 2026-03-14

Overview

BADGRTechnologies LLC ("we", "our", "us") built BADGR Bolt as a commercial Android application. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is handled.

If you use BADGR Bolt as a free user without creating an account, no personal data leaves your device. All reading content and progress are stored locally. This policy primarily governs data handling for users who create an account or purchase a Pro subscription.

Data We Collect

Email address
Required to create and authenticate your BADGR Bolt account via Firebase Authentication.
Reading progress
Your current position in each book is synced to the cloud for Pro subscribers to enable cross-device continuity.
Book metadata
Book title, file type, and word count are synced for Pro subscribers. Book content is never uploaded or stored on our servers.
Crash logs
Anonymised crash reports are collected via Firebase Crashlytics to help us identify and fix bugs. These do not contain personally identifiable information.
Usage analytics
Anonymous, aggregated usage patterns are collected via Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used. No individual user is identified.
File conversion
When you import a PDF or EPUB, the file is sent to our conversion server to extract text. The file and extracted text are processed in memory and are not stored on our servers after the response is returned.

Data We Do Not Collect

BADGR Bolt does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

Book content Payment information Location data Device identifiers Contacts or calendar Camera or microphone Browsing history Biometric data

Payment processing for Pro subscriptions and one-time purchases is handled entirely by Google Play. We never receive or store your payment details.

How We Use Your Data

Authentication

Your email address is used solely to create and manage your account. We do not use it for marketing without your explicit consent.

Cloud Sync (Pro)

Reading progress and book metadata are synced to Firebase Firestore to restore your library when you sign in on another device or reinstall the app.

Stability and Improvement

Crash logs and anonymous analytics help us identify bugs and prioritise features. No personally identifiable data is included in these reports.

Third-Party Services

BADGR Bolt uses the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy.

Firebase Auth
Account authentication. Firebase Privacy Policy
Firestore
Cloud storage for Pro user library and progress data.
Crashlytics
Anonymous crash reporting.
Firebase Analytics
Anonymous usage analytics. Google Privacy Policy
Google Play Billing
Subscription and one-time purchase processing. Google Play Terms
BADGR Text Service
Our own PDF/EPUB conversion backend. Files are processed in memory and not retained.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your account data (email, synced books, reading progress) is retained for as long as your account exists. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the address below. Upon deletion, all Firestore data associated with your user ID is permanently removed.

Crash logs and analytics data are anonymised and cannot be individually attributed or deleted.

Children's Privacy

BADGR Bolt is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Security

All data transmitted between BADGR Bolt and our services is encrypted in transit using TLS. Data stored in Firebase Firestore is encrypted at rest by Google. We follow security best practices in our development process, including scoped database access rules that prevent any user from accessing another user's data.

Android Permissions

INTERNET
Required for Firebase services, cloud sync, and file conversion. Not used when the app is operating in fully offline mode.

BADGR Bolt requests only the single permission listed above. No permissions for location, camera, microphone, contacts, or storage are requested.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app adds new features or services. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of BADGR Bolt after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
