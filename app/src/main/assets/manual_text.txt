Welcome to BADGR Bolt!

BADGR Bolt is a precision speed reading app that uses RSVP (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation) and ORP (Optimal Recognition Point) to help you read faster with better comprehension.

Getting Started:
When you open BADGR Bolt, you will see the main navigation bar at the bottom of the screen with four tabs: Library, Stats, Settings, and Account.

Adding Your First Book:
1. Go to the Library tab.
2. Tap the + button in the bottom-right corner.
3. Choose your file format (TXT, PDF, EPUB, DOCX, or IMAGE).
4. Select your document from your device or cloud storage.
5. Once imported, tap the book in your library to start reading.

Reading Controls:
- WPM: Adjust your reading speed from 60 to 1200 WPM.
- Words at a time: Choose 1-4 words per frame.
- Punctuation pauses: Natural rhythm with automatic pauses.

Pro Features:
Upgrade to Bolt Pro for cloud sync, unlimited library, and advanced stats. Check the Account tab for more details.

Enjoy your reading journey with BADGR Bolt!
