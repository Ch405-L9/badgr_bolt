# Production Access Questionnaire Answers - BADGR Bolt

These answers are based on the "Production Access Questionnaire" (com.badgr.orbreader_production.pdf) and the improvements made to the app.

### 1) How did you recruit users for your closed test?
We used a paid testing provider to gather comprehensive feedback on BADGR Bolt's performance. Additionally, we reached out to our target audience of students and avid readers to obtain valuable insights from real users, enhancing the app's usability and features.

### 2) How easy was it to recruit testers for your app?
**Answer:** Easy.
The value proposition of speed reading resonated well with our target audience, making it straightforward to find engaged testers.

### 3) Describe the engagement you received from testers during your closed test.
Testers actively engaged by providing constructive feedback on features, usability, and overall experience. Their input was instrumental in refining the app, leading to crucial improvements in user navigation, interface design, and feature accessibility, significantly boosting user satisfaction.

### 4) Provide a summary of the feedback that you received from testers. Include how you collected the feedback.
Feedback highlighted the need for a dynamic walkthrough for new users, improved ASO optimization, enhanced screenshots reflecting key features, and a "Rate Your App" button. We collected feedback through surveys, direct communication, and usability testing sessions to ensure comprehensive insights.

### 5) Who is the intended audience for your app?
BADGR Bolt is tailored for students, professionals, and avid readers seeking to enhance their reading speed and comprehension. The app serves anyone looking to efficiently manage their reading materials and improve their focus using innovative RSVP technology.

### 6) Describe how your app provides value to the users.
BADGR Bolt empowers users to read 2x to 5x faster through its RSVP technology, allowing for deeper focus and retention. With offline support, customizable features, and a distraction-free interface, it transforms reading into a seamless and efficient experience for all users.

### 7) How many installs do you expect your app to have in your first year?
**Answer:** 10k - 100k.

### 8) What changes did you make to your app based on what you learned during your closed test?
In response to user feedback, we implemented a dynamic walkthrough for new users, optimized the app description for ASO, enhanced screenshots to showcase features, and added a "Rate Your App" button, significantly improving user onboarding and engagement with the app.

### 9) How did you decide that your app is ready for production?
After thorough testing and integrating user feedback, we ensured BADGR Bolt is stable, user-friendly, and feature-rich. The app has undergone rigorous enhancements to address usability issues, making it optimized for a successful launch on Google Play.

### 10) What did you do differently this time?
This time, we prioritized user engagement by actively seeking feedback from a diverse group of testers. We focused on refining the user experience based on their insights, which led to a more polished application that meets the needs of our audience effectively.
